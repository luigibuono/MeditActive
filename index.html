About us

Mission & Vision

Where did the idea come from:

The level of self-awareness in society is very low. The world has become fast, optimization outweighs introspection in importance. This app was created with the intention of refocusing on oneself and introducing practices such as meditation into everyday life.

Vision

Helping people to live peacefully with themselves and achieve their goals.

Mission

Helping people in their daily lives by guiding them on a meditation path.

Who we are?

The first co-founder has been practicing meditation for 20 years and teaching it for 17. Together with the co-founder, project manager for 16 years, he created this app. The team is made up of 15 people who work remotely around Italy.

How does it work?

The app was developed to bring the benefits of meditation and coordinate it with those of personal growth, to achieve various goals: daily, monthly and annual. By entering your data and current habits, it is possible to select a series of objectives to achieve and the time frame chosen by the user. For each goal you earn virtual coins that can be used to plant trees or contribute to fundraising for projects with social impact or for the planet. The company relies exclusively on servers powered by renewable energy.
